Here's some of the other coding projects I'm currently working on:

FLAB

This is a blog for college football enthusiasts who also like craft beer. Merritt Schumann II is the founding father of this group and has used his ingenious Excel skills over the years to organize his own approach to a fantasy football league where winners are awarded.... you guessed it... their choice of beer. Yes; he usually wins. I plead the 5th regarding how much I help him enjoy the spoils.

DEVinition

One New DEV seeks to define life's challenges and experiences entering the world of code. I read somewhere that it is good practice to try and replicate the website designs of others. The landing page of this site is inspired by a blog which made a top ten list for its design appeal.

GV Wine Country

Did you know that there are 21 wineries on the Western Slope in Colorado? I haven't been to all of them, but I'm creating this app so that users can plan their winery tours whether by bike (which is popular), Uber, or limo! I'm currently investigating some wine APIs I've found, and will likely integrate 3rd party apps such as Yelp and Google Maps. The design utilizes Bootstrap 4 and currently contains a parallax feature. As for wine, would you rather have red or white?

Ponder

Did you know that founders of the multiple intelligence theory suggest that over our lifetime that we usually develop our strengths in two to three areas primarily? Where you do you think your intelligence strengths reside? Do you think you were born with these strengths, develop them over time, or both? This website is a resource site intended to help those who work with youth be strategic about playtime as a resource for discovering latent strengths & responding to ignited interest.

FoodieApp

Have you found "the way" when it comes to healthy eating? But then, the path to eating healthy is hard to navigate and wayyyy toooo tempting when it comes to dining out?!?! Foodie Path will help you find the restaurants with the menu options you seek so that your path remains clean and pure. This is a 3 model, sessions-based, MEAN stack app which calls on the YELP API. Additionally, this is a collaborative project between people building their dev skills & who also call themselves foodies!

D51 GT Insider

Where do you feel led to volunteer your time? I'm just ending four years of service on a local committee which reported to a school board with advisory recommendations on matters related to gifted youth in our community. I served as a Co-Chair for two of those years, and during that time created a Google Site for the committee as a capstone project while working on my master's degree to obtain my certification as a Gifted-talented Education Specialist. This project is a redesign of that original site. During my service on the committee, I saw a need for a digital presence so that transparency, communication, outreach, and collaboration could better be served. This project is part of a torch I'm passing on as I move on to other volunteer commitments.

My task list includes adding a link to the Github repo & Heroku site.
